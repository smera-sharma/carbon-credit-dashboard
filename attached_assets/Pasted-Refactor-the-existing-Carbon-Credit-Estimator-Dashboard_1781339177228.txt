## 3. Carbon Credit Simulator

Purpose: Estimate future carbon credit potential.

Allow users to modify:

* Farm Area (hectares)
* Soil Depth (cm)

Default values:

* Farm Area = 5 hectares
* Soil Depth = 30 cm

Use the formula:

Carbon Stock = SOC_target × Farm Area × (Soil Depth / 30)

Carbon Credits = Carbon Stock × 3.67

Display:

* Estimated Carbon Stock
* Estimated Carbon Credits
* Estimated CO₂ Equivalent

Charts:

* Carbon credits vs Farm Area.
* Top 10 samples with the highest carbon credit potential.

Add scenario-based insights explaining how changing farm area or soil depth affects the estimates.

This page should focus ONLY on carbon estimation and simulation and should NOT repeat soil analysis metrics.
